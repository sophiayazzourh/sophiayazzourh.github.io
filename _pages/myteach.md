*2021–2024*

- Led tutorials and practical sessions for undergraduate students (Years 1–3, IUT GMP).
- Courses covered:
  - Calculus and differential/integral analysis  
  - Applied linear algebra (matrices, linear systems)  
  - Probability and statistics applied to mechanical engineering  
  - Numerical methods and optimization  
  - Introduction to algorithmics and numerical modelling  
- Delivered lectures and tutorial sessions in **English** for the international track.  
- Designed and translated teaching materials (tutorials, practical exercises) **from French to English**.
